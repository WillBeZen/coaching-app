# PaceCoach — Athlete Guide

## Getting Started

### Sign Up
1. Open the app and click **Create Account**.
2. Enter your name and the email address (remember which email you used as the coach needs this to add you to their roster).
3. Choose a password, select **Athlete** as your role, and click **Create Account**.
4. On the next screen you can optionally enter your current Personal Bests — you can skip this and add them later.

### Sign In
Enter your email and password and click **Sign In**.

---

## Viewing Your Schedule

### Week View
The week view shows your training plan day by day. Use the **← →** arrows to move between weeks.

Each session card shows:
- Session type (colour-coded badge)
- Title and distance/duration
- Your coach's notes

### List View
Switch to **List** using the tab at the top for a scrollable view of upcoming and past sessions.

---

## Logging a Session

When you complete a session, tap **Log** on the session card.

You can record:
- **Actual distance** and **duration**
- **Rating** — 1 to 5 stars (rating 4 or 5 automatically celebrates it on the group feed!)
- **Notes** — how it felt, any issues, etc.

Logged sessions are marked as complete and visible to your coach.

---

## Logging Extra Sessions

Did a parkrun? Hit the gym? You can log sessions you did outside your coach's plan so they can see your full training load.

1. Click **+ Log Session** on your home screen.
2. Fill in the type, date, distance, and any notes.
3. Save — the session appears in your week view with a purple **EXTRA** badge.

---

## Races

### Viewing Races
Go to the **Races** tab to see all upcoming and past races your coach has scheduled for you.

### Adding Your Own Race
1. On the Races tab, click **+ Add Race**.
2. Fill in the race name, date, event/distance, and an optional target time.
3. Save — it appears with a gold **MY RACE** badge so your coach can see you added it yourself.

### Logging a Race Result
After a race, click **Log Result** on the race card.
- Enter your finish time and any notes.
- If it's a new PB or Season Best, it updates automatically and posts a celebration to your group feed!

---

## Records (PBs & Season Bests)

Go to the **Records** tab to see your Personal Bests and Season Bests for each distance.

### Adding or Editing a Record
1. Click any event row in the Records tab.
2. Enter your PB time and date, and/or your SB time and date.
3. Save.

Records also update automatically when you log a race result that's faster than your current best.

---

## Marking Unavailability

Going on holiday? Feeling ill? Let your coach know so they don't schedule sessions on those days.

1. Click **+ Mark Unavailable** on your home screen.
2. Select the date.
3. To mark multiple days, tick **Multi-day** and set an end date.
4. Add an optional reason (e.g. "Holiday", "Illness").
5. Save.

Your coach will see a red banner on those days in the week view and get a warning if they try to schedule a session anyway.

### Managing Unavailability Periods
Your current unavailability periods are listed in the **Records** tab. Click any entry to edit or duplicate it, or use the delete button to remove it.

---

## Activity Feed

The Feed is where your coach posts announcements and where achievements get celebrated automatically.

### Viewing the Feed
Go to the **Feed** tab. Use the tab strip to switch between **All** posts and individual group feeds.

### Reacting to Posts
Click 🏎️ ⚡ 🔥 or 🏆 under any post to react. Click again to remove your reaction.

### Group Controls
In the Feed tab, you'll see controls for each group you're in:

- **Mute** — you stay in the group but stop seeing other athletes' posts (your coach's posts still come through).
- **Leave** — you exit the group entirely. Past posts from that group will no longer be visible to you.

---

## Distance Units

Tap the units toggle in the top bar to switch between **km** and **miles**. The setting is saved on your device.

---

## Tips

- Rate your sessions honestly — 4 and 5 star sessions automatically post to your group feed so your coach and teammates can celebrate with you.
- Log your extra sessions so your coach has a complete picture of your training load when planning your next week.
- Mark unavailability in advance — it saves your coach from having to reschedule sessions later.
