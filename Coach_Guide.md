# PaceCoach — Coach Guide

## Getting Started

### Sign Up
1. Open the app and click **Register**.
2. Enter your name, email, and password, then select **Coach** as your role.
3. Click **Create Account** — you'll be signed in immediately.

### Sign In
Enter your email and password and click **Sign In**. Ka-chow.

---

## Adding Athletes

1. Click **+ Add Athlete** in the left sidebar.
2. Enter the athlete's name and email. They'll need to register themselves using that same email address.
3. Once they register, they appear in your sidebar and you can begin adding sessions.

To remove an athlete from your roster, click their name in the sidebar, then use the **Remove Athlete** button on their profile.

---

## Planning Sessions

### Creating a Session
1. Select an athlete (or "All Athletes") from the sidebar.
2. Switch to **Week** or **List** view using the tabs at the top.
3. Click **+ Session** (or click directly on a day in the week grid).
4. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — e.g. "Easy 8km"
   - **Type** — Easy, Tempo, Intervals, Long, Recovery, Race, Strength, Cross-training
   - **Date**, **Distance**, **Duration**, **Pace target**, **Heart rate zone**
   - **Notes** — coaching cues, warm-up instructions, etc.
5. Click **Save Session**.

If the athlete has marked that date as unavailable, a warning dialog will appear. You can choose to proceed or pick a different date.

### Duplicating a Session
Click the **Duplicate** icon on any session card to copy it. Useful for repeating weekly structures.

### Deleting a Session
Click the **Delete** (trash) icon on the session card and confirm.

---

## Viewing Athlete Weeks

- Use the **← →** arrows to move between weeks.
- Each day column shows the sessions scheduled for that athlete.
- **Red banner** on a day = the athlete has marked themselves unavailable.
- **Purple card** = a session the athlete logged themselves (outside your plan).

---

## Races

### Adding a Race
1. Select the athlete.
2. Go to the **Races** tab.
3. Click **+ Race**, fill in the race name, date, event/distance, and optional target time.
4. Save — the race appears as *Upcoming*.

### Logging a Result
1. On a completed race, click **Log Result**.
2. Enter the actual time and any notes.
3. Save — the race moves to *Completed* and PBs/SBs update automatically if the time is faster.

---

## Records (PBs & Season Bests)

- Go to the **Records** tab for any athlete.
- You can see their PB and SB for each standard event (800m through Marathon).
- PBs update automatically when a race result is logged — or you can edit them manually by clicking the event row.

---

## Training Groups

Groups let you organise athletes into squads and post targeted announcements.

### Creating a Group
1. Click **+ New Group** at the bottom of the sidebar.
2. Give the group a name, pick a colour, and choose an emoji.
3. Click **Save**.

### Managing Members
1. Click the **⚙** (settings) icon next to the group name in the sidebar.
2. Use **+ Add** to add athletes and **Remove** to remove them.
3. Athletes who have left a group are automatically excluded from the list.

### Editing a Group
Click the **✎** (pencil) icon next to the group name to rename it, change colour or emoji.

### Filtering by Group
Click a group name in the sidebar to filter the main view to only that group's athletes.

---

## Activity Feed

The Feed is a shared space for announcements, celebrations, and group updates.

### Posting
1. Go to the **Feed** tab.
2. Type your message in the compose box.
3. Choose a target from the dropdown:
   - **All** — visible to your whole roster
   - A specific group name — visible to that group only
   - An athlete's name (private message)
4. Click **Post**.

### Auto-Posts
The feed automatically celebrates athlete achievements:
- New PB or Season Best
- Race completed
- Session rated 4 or 5 stars by the athlete

### Reactions
Click any of the emoji buttons (🏎️ ⚡ 🔥 🏆) below a post to react. Click again to remove your reaction.

### Deleting a Post
Click the **✕** button on any post to delete it (you can delete any post on your roster's feed).

### Group Tabs
Use the tab strip at the top of the Feed to filter between **All** posts and individual group feeds.

---

## Distance Units

Toggle between **km** and **miles** using the units button in the top bar. The setting is saved per-device and affects all distances, paces, and chart labels throughout the app.

---

## Tips

- Sessions scheduled on an athlete's unavailable dates show a warning — check the week view for red banners before building a training week.
- Athletes can log their own extra sessions (parkrun, gym, etc.) — these show as purple cards so you can see full training load.
- PBs and SBs update automatically when you log a race result — no need to edit them separately.
